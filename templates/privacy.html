Privacy Policy

Information Collection

We collect minimal personal information necessary for account creation and service functionality:

Data Usage and Protection

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

  • All uploaded images are automatically deleted after processing

  • We do not share or sell any user data to third parties

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Request a copy of your data

  • Maintain your session security
  • Remember your login status
  • Improve website functionality
